FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What type of visa do I need to move to Australia?

    The type of visa depends on your purpose, whether it's work, study, joining a partner or family, or settling permanently. Our migration agents can assess your situation and recommend the most suitable visa option.

  • How long does the visa application process take?

    Processing times vary by visa type and individual circumstances. Some visas may take a few weeks, while others can take several months. We help you stay informed and ensure all documents are submitted correctly to avoid delays..

  • Can you guarantee my visa will be approved?

    No agent can guarantee approval, as the final decision lies with the Department of Home Affairs. However, we significantly improve your chances by preparing a strong, complete, and accurate application.

  • What happens during the Free Discovery Call?

    During the call, we assess your eligibility, answer your questions, and guide you on the next steps. If you qualify, we offer full application services to support you throughout the visa process.
